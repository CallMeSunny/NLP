Pictured: The moment FOUR illegal Chinese immigrants are found crammed into car trunk on US-Mexico border amid failed smuggling attempt
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-4323880/Four-illegal-Chinese-immigrants-inside-car-trunk.html
 13:24 BST, 17 March 2017 
Customs and Border Protection agents have arrested an American citizen at the US-Mexico border after discovering four undocumented Chinese immigrants stuffed into the trunk of his sedan.According to a press release from the federal agency, the incident took place at around 5.45pm on Tuesday at the San Ysidro port of entry in San Diego, where officers stopped a 2014 white Chrysler 200.While the sedan was at the booth for inspection, a roving CBP canine team's dog, which is trained to detect narcotic and humans, alerted its handlers to the presence of some suspicious cargo.         Like sardines in a can: This image taken by Customs and Border Protection agents at the US-Mexico border shows four undocumented Chinese immigrants stuffed inside the trunk of a Chrysler sedan during a botched smuggling attemptOfficers then opened the trunk and found three women and a man crammed inside the small storage space.According to US News and World Report's Best Card Deals rubric on its website, a 2014 Chrysler 200 has a trunk volume of 13.6 cubic feet.All four stowaways were removed from the vehicle and led to a secure location for further processing, at which point it was determined that they were Chinese nationals who were in the US illegally.The driver of the Chrysler, a 24-year-old man whose name has not been released, was booked into Metropolitan Correctional Center to await arraignment on federal charges related to human smuggling.
