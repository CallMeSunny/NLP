The air quality, temperature and humidity inside the dome are strictly monitored.A filter system is said to be installed in the inflatable hall so that pupils could breathe in clean air.The reading of PM2.5, air particulate matters that damage lungs, is 19 inside the gym. This is compared to a reading of 320 outdoors, according to a report on the local news outlet, hebnews.cn, on December 5.Despite the alarming pollution outside, all students in the school can attend physical education lessons as usual inside the new sports hall, the article claimed.The movable stadium consists of a tennis court and a badminton court. Pupils can also run, skip ropes, kick shuttlecocks on the playground.While its walls and ceiling are made of inflatable materials, the dome's floor appear no different from other sports stadiums.The school principal said electricity was the main expense of the gym. It consumes 50 kilowatt-hour (kWh) a day.
