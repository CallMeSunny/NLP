China's Xi says hopes Duterte's visit can fully improve ties By Reuters   Published: 05:14 BST, 20 October 2016  |  Updated: 05:14 BST, 20 October 2016                e-mail         BEIJING, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping said on Thursday he hoped Philippine President Rodrigo Duterte's visit to China could help fully improve ties and that the two countries could appropriately handle disputes.Duterte told Xi during a meeting at China's Great Hall of the People that the roots of the two countries' bonds were deep and not easily severed.The two sides are involved in a territorial dispute in the South China Sea. (Reporting by Ben Blanchard, Writing by Michael Martina; Editing by Nick Macfie)
