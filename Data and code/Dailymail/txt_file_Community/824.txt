China pledges $2 bln to help development of poor countries By Reuters   Published: 16:03 BST, 26 September 2015  |  Updated: 16:03 BST, 26 September 2015                e-mail         UNITED NATIONS, Sept 26 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping announced on Saturday that Beijing will establish an assistance fund with an initial pledge of $2 billion to help developing countries implement a global sustainable development agenda over the next 15 years."China will continue to increase investment in the least developed countries, aiming to increase its total to $12 billion by 2030," Xi told a sustainable development summit of world leaders at the United Nations."China will exempt the debt of the outstanding intergovernmental interest-free loans due by the end of 2015 owed by the relevant least developed countries, landlocked developing countries and small island developing countries," he added. (Reporting by Michelle Nichols, editing by Louis Charbonneau)
