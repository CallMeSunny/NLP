Trump congratulates China's Xi on "extraordinary elevation"
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-5017801/Trump-congratulates-Chinas-Xi-extraordinary-elevation.html
 20:40 BST, 25 October 2017 
 WASHINGTON, Oct 25 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump said on Wednesday he spoke to Chinese President Xi Jinping to congratulate him on a new leadership lineup unveiled at the Chinese ruling Communist Party Congress. The two also discussed North Korea and trade, Trump said in a Twitter post. "Spoke to President Xi of China to congratulate him on his extraordinary elevation. Also discussed NoKo &amp; trade, two very important subjects!" he wrote. (Reporting by Doina Chiacu; Editing by David Alexander)
