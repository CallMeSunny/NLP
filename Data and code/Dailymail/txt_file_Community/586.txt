Trump confirms he won't meet with China's Xi before March 1 trade deadline
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-6680019/Trump-confirms-wont-meet-Chinas-Xi-March-1-trade-deadline.html
 20:03 BST, 7 February 2019 
 WASHINGTON, Feb 7 (Reuters) - President Donald Trump confirmed on Thursday that he did not plan to meet with Chinese President Xi Jinping before a March 1 deadline set by the two countries to achieve a trade deal. Asked by a reporter whether there would be a meeting before the deadline, Trump said: "No." When asked whether there would be a meeting in the next month or so, Trump said: "Not yet. Maybe." (Reporting by Jeff Mason and Alexandra Alper Editing by James Dalgleish)
