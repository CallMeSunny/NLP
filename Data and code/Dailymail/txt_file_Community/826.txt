French President Hollande to visit China next week By Afp   Published: 21:20 BST, 26 October 2015  |  Updated: 21:20 BST, 26 October 2015                e-mail         French President Francois Hollande will make a state visit to China on November 2-3 at the invitation of his Chinese counterpart Xi Jinping, with climate change high on the agenda, Hollande's office announced Monday.The French leader will also visit South Korea on a visit marking the 130th anniversary of French-Korean diplomatic ties.Hollande announced last month that he planned to visit China in order to launch a bilateral appeal "that we work to make a success of the climate conference," to be held from November 30-December 11 in Paris.
