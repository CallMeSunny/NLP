Obama tells Xi: U.S., China have big stake in each other's success By Reuters   Published: 03:48 BST, 12 November 2014  |  Updated: 03:48 BST, 12 November 2014                e-mail         BEIJING, Nov 12 (Reuters) - U.S. President Barack Obama told Chinese President Xi Jinping on Wednesday that he welcomes a China that is peaceful, prosperous and stable.Obama added that China and the United States have important differences, but he was encouraged by Xi's willingness to engage constructively, and that the two countries have an enormous stake in each other's success."There are important differences that we have...but what I've been very encouraged by is your willingness, Mr. President, to engage in a constructive dialogue," Obama said at the start of formal talks between the two in Beijing. (Reporting by Matt Spetalnick; Writing by Ben Blanchard; Editing by Raju Gopalakrishnan)
