Xi says China has reduced iron and steel capacity By Reuters   Published: 16:18 BST, 21 October 2015  |  Updated: 16:18 BST, 21 October 2015                e-mail         LONDON, Oct 21 (Reuters) - China has reduced capacity in its iron and steel industry by 700 million tonnes, President Xi Jinping said on Wednesday."China has taken a series of steps to reduce capacity, we have reduced more than 700 million tonnes of production capacity and you can just imagine our task of finding jobs for those workers," Xi said through a translator.It was not immediately possible to verify the figure or the accuracy of the translation. (Reporting by Guy Faulconbridge; Editing by Pravin Char)
