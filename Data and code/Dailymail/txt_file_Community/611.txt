China's Xi says hopes for fair Australia investment policy By Reuters   Published: 03:39 BST, 4 September 2016  |  Updated: 03:39 BST, 4 September 2016                e-mail         BEIJING, Sept 4 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping told Australian Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull on Sunday that he hopes Australia continues to dedicate itself to providing a fair, transparent and predictable policy environment for foreign investors, state media said.The two were speaking on the sidelines of a G20 summit in the eastern Chinese city of Hangzhou.China was angered after Australia last month blocked the A$10 billion ($7.7 billion) sale of the country's biggest energy grid to Chinese bidders. (Reporting by Ben Blanchard; Editing by Robert Birsel)
