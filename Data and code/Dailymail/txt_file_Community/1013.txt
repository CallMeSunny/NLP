(AP Photo/Wong Maye-E) China stands ready to work for further improvements in bilateral relations and to make "positive contributions to safeguarding peace, stability and development in the region," Xi wrote.China fought for North Korea in the 1950-53 Korean War and ties were close for decades afterward. However, North Korea has rejected China's calls for economic reforms and Kim has yet to visit China, despite its status as the North's key source of financial and diplomatic support.China has also criticized North Korea's nuclear and missile tests and endorsed United Nations Security Council sanctions, recently announcing a further tightening of trade restrictions on Kim's impoverished communist country.
