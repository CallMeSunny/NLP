Japan says Trump-Xi call is positive for region -spokesman By Reuters   Published: 15:24 BST, 10 February 2017  |  Updated: 15:24 BST, 10 February 2017                e-mail         WASHINGTON, Feb 10 (Reuters) - Japan views U.S. President Donald Trump's call with Chinese President Xi Jinping as a good and positive step, a spokesman for Japan said on Friday, following the U.S-China call Thursday night."It's good and positive in terms of peace and security of the region and the international community," Norio Maruyama told reporters during a news conference."We consider that it is constructive and frank discussions on the terms relating to the peace and security in the regions. This is the right way forward."(Reporting by Steve Holland; Writing by Susan Heavey; Editing by Bernadette Baum)
