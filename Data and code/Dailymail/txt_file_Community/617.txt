China's Xi tells Trump cooperation is only choice By Reuters   Published: 04:18 BST, 14 November 2016  |  Updated: 04:18 BST, 14 November 2016                e-mail         BEIJING, Nov 14 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping told U.S. President-elect Donald Trump in a phone call on Monday that cooperation was the only choice for relations between the two countries, Chinese state media said.Trump had taken a tough stance on China in the run-up to the U.S. presidential election, saying that he would take aggressive action against Beijing on trade issues. (Reporting by Michael Martina and Sue-Lin Wong; Editing by Paul Tait)
