Chinese general says China island building will not affect freedom of navigation By Reuters   Published: 02:41 BST, 17 October 2015  |  Updated: 02:41 BST, 17 October 2015                e-mail        19 shares    BEIJING, Oct 17 (Reuters) - China's building activities in the South China Sea will not affect freedom of navigation and China will never recklessly resort to use of force, senior Chinese General Fan Changlong said on Saturday.Fan, one of the vice chairmen of the Central Military Commission, which President Xi Jinping heads and which controls the Chinese armed forces, made the comments at a forum in Beijing. (Reporting by Ben Blanchard, Writing by Michael Martina; Editing by Michael Perry)
