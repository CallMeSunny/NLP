Trump on G20: 'China wants to make a deal; if we can make a deal we will.'
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-6418765/Trump-G20-China-wants-make-deal-make-deal-will.html
 15:49 BST, 22 November 2018 
 PALM BEACH, Florida, Nov 22 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump said on Thursday he hopes he can make a deal with China when he meets President Xi Jinping at the G20 meeting in Argentina next week. "I can say this, China wants to make a deal very badly - because of the tariffs," Trump told reporters in Palm Beach, Florida. "China wants to make a deal; if we can make a deal, we will." (Reporting by David Brunnstrom and Roberta Rampton Editing by Nick Zieminski)
