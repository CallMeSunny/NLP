White House adviser says China-U.S. trade deal still possible
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-6434989/White-House-adviser-says-China-U-S-trade-deal-possible.html
 18:59 BST, 27 November 2018 
 WASHINGTON, Nov 27 (Reuters) - White House economic adviser Larry Kudlow said on Tuesday that U.S. President Donald Trump will have a dinner meeting with Chinese President Xi Jinping at the upcoming G20 gathering in Argentina and also held open the possibility that the two countries would reach a trade deal. "There is a good possibility that we can make a deal and he is open to it," Kudlow, the National Economic Council director, told a press briefing, referring to Trump. (Reporting by Roberta Rampton Writing by Lisa Lambert)
