China's Xi meets North Korean delegation - Xinhua By Reuters   Published: 09:57 BST, 1 June 2016  |  Updated: 09:58 BST, 1 June 2016                e-mail         BEIJING, June 1 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping on Wednesday met a visiting delegation from North Korea's ruling party, China's Xinhua state news agency said, without providing other details.Senior North Korean diplomat Ri Su Yong is visiting China.Ri was the country's foreign minister until he was named a member of the politburo during a recent congress of North Korea's ruling Workers' Party.(Reporting by Ben Blanchard; Editing by Robert Birsel)
