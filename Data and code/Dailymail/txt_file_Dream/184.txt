President Xi says China should not focus on growth rate only By Reuters   Published: 02:45 BST, 28 March 2015  |  Updated: 02:45 BST, 28 March 2015                e-mail         BOAO, China, March 28 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping said on Saturday that the country should not focus on its economic growth rate only.He was speaking at a forum in Boao on the southern Chinese island of Hainan.Chinese leaders have announced an economic growth target of around 7 percent for this year, below the 7.5 percent goal in 2014. (Reporting by Adam Jourdan; Writing by Ben Blanchard; Editing by Michael Perry)
