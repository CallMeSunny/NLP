Trump says all must join together to stop arming and financing the N.Korea regime
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-5065045/Trump-says-join-stop-arming-financing-N-Korea-regime.html
 07:21 BST, 9 November 2017 
 BEIJING, Nov 9 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump on Thursday said all parties must join together to stop arming and financing the North Korea regime, adding that the United States and China have agreed not to replicate failed approaches from the past on dealing with the crisis. In concluding remarks in Beijing following meetings with Chinese President Xi Jinping, Trump said it will require collective action and strength to win the peace. (Reporting by Steve Holland and Lusha Zhang; Editing by Nick Macfie)
