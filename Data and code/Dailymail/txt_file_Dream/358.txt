White House official: North Korea is test for U.S.-China relations
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-4380664/White-House-official-North-Korea-test-U-S-China-relations.html
 22:41 BST, 4 April 2017 
WASHINGTON, April 4 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump will discuss how to rein in North Korea with Chinese President Xi Jinping later this week in what a senior White House official said on Tuesday was a test for the U.S.-Chinese relationship.Briefing reporters on the Trump-Xi meetings, the official said the president sees the meeting as the first step in a "results-oriented relationship" and that he wants economic ties that are fair, balanced and based on reciprocity. (Reporting by Steve Holland and Roberta Rampton; Editing by Sandra Maler)
