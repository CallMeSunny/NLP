Kerry says China's Xi believes NGO law will be applied fairly By Reuters   Published: 13:46 BST, 7 June 2016  |  Updated: 13:46 BST, 7 June 2016                e-mail         BEIJING, June 7 (Reuters) - China intends to remain open and does not believe a new law on foreign non-government bodies will affect its ability to do so, U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry on Tuesday quoted Chinese President Xi Jinping as saying during talks.Kerry was speaking to reporters at the end of high-level discussions between China and the United States in Beijing. (Reporting by Yeganeh Torbati; Writing by Ben Blanchard; Editing by Clarence Fernandez)
