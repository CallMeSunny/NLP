Obama calls Xi to praise China's role in climate talks -W.House By Reuters   Published: 15:15 BST, 14 December 2015  |  Updated: 15:15 BST, 14 December 2015                e-mail         WASHINGTON, Dec 14 (Reuters) - U.S. President Barack Obama spoke by telephone Sunday night with Chinese President Xi Jinping to express appreciation for the important role China played in securing a historic climate agreement in Paris, the White House said.The leaders recognized that close coordination of their negotiating teams helped secure Saturday's agreement, it said in a statement released on Monday."The president emphasized the importance of continuing close U.S.-China cooperation on climate change issues into the future," the White House said. (Reporting by Doina Chiacu; Editing by Susan Heavey)
