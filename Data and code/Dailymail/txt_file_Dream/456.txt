White House not expected to announce Trump-Xi summit date -official
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-6886517/White-House-not-expected-announce-Trump-Xi-summit-date-official.html
 15:00 BST, 4 April 2019 
 WASHINGTON, April 4 (Reuters) - The White House is not expected to announce a date on Thursday for a meeting between President Donald Trump and Chinese President Xi Jinping on trade, an administration official said, denying a report by the Wall Street Journal. "The White House is not expected to announce a date for a meeting," the official said. Trump is scheduled to meet with China's top trade negotiator, Liu He, at 4:30 p.m. (2030 GMT) at the White House after another day of talks between top trade officials from both countries. (Reporting by Jeff Mason)
