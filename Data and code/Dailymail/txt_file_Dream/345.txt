Trump says "big progress" being made on possible deal with China
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-6538129/Trump-says-big-progress-possible-deal-China.html
 16:15 BST, 29 December 2018 
 WASHINGTON, Dec 29 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump said on Twitter on Saturday that he had a "long and very good call" with Chinese President Xi Jinping and that a possible trade deal between the United States and China was progressing well. "Just had a long and very good call with President Xi of China," Trump wrote. "Deal is moving along very well. If made, it will be very comprehensive, covering all subjects, areas and points of dispute. Big progress being made!" (Reporting by Yeganeh Torbati; Editing by Daniel Wallis)
