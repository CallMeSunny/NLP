Xi says China's investments in Latin America to hit $250 bln in next 10 yrs By Reuters   Published: 02:39 BST, 8 January 2015  |  Updated: 02:39 BST, 8 January 2015                e-mail         BEIJING, Jan 8 (Reuters) - Chinese President Xi Jinping said on Thursday that China's investments in Latin America will hit $250 billion in the next 10 years and two-way trade is estimated to rise to $500 billion during the same period.Xi was addressing leaders of the Community of Latin American and Carribean States, or CELAC - a bloc of 33 countries in the region that excludes the United States and Canada - who gathered in Beijing for the first time for a two-day forum on Thursday. (Reporting by Megha Rajagopalan, Writing by Sui-Lee Wee; Editing by Jacqueline Wong)
