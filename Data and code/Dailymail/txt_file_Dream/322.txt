China president urges faster reforms to unleash growth drivers By Reuters   Published: 12:02 BST, 25 May 2016  |  Updated: 12:02 BST, 25 May 2016                e-mail         BEIJING, May 25 (Reuters) - China must press ahead with "supply-side structural reforms" to help unleash growth drivers in the economy, state radio quoted President Xi Jinping as saying on Wednesday.Such structural reforms will help boost China's industrial structures and productivity, Xi was quoted as saying during a trip to China's northeastern province of Heilongjiang.China may "miss an opportunity" if policies and reforms are not persistent, Xi said. (Reporting by China Monitoring Desk and Kevin Yao; Editing by Jacqueline Wong)
