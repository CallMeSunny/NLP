China's Xi supports India's aspiration for greater UN role By Reuters   Published: 12:46 BST, 18 September 2014  |  Updated: 12:46 BST, 18 September 2014                e-mail         NEW DELHI, Sept 18 (Reuters) - China supports India's aspiration to play a greater role at the United Nations, including on the Security Council, President Xi Jinping said in a speech on Thursday after a summit with Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi.India has no permanent seat on the Security Council despite being home to a seventh of the world's population. China is one of the council's five permanent members, together with the United States, Russia, Britain and France. (Reporting by Manoj Kumar and Sanjeev Miglani; Writing by Douglas Busvine; editing by Malini Menon)
