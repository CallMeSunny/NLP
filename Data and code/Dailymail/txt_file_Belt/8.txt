Britain "committed to helping China realize Belt and Road"
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/reuters/article-6962763/Britain-committed-helping-China-realize-Belt-Road.html
 08:47 BST, 26 April 2019 
 BEIJING, April 26 (Reuters) - Britain is committed to help realize the potential of China's Belt and Road Initiative (BRI), British finance minister Philip Hammond said on Friday. Speaking at a summit in Beijing on China's programme to re-create the old Silk Road joining China with Asia and Europe, Hammond said the BRI must work for everyone for it to turn into a sustainable reality and offered British expertise in project financing. (Reporting by Brenda Goh; writing by Beijing Monitoring Desk; Editing by Nick Macfie)
