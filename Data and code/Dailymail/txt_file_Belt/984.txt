Up to four killed in suicide blast in Yemen's Aden-witnesses By Reuters   Published: 06:55 BST, 12 April 2016  |  Updated: 06:55 BST, 12 April 2016                e-mail         ADEN, April 12 (Reuters) - A suicide bomber detonated an explosives-laden belt near a football stadium in the southern Yemeni port city of Aden on Tuesday, killing up to four people, witnesses said.Eight people were wounded in the attack, which appeared to target young men queuing to register to serve in the military, they said. They said up to four people were killed, apart from the bomber. (Reporting by Mohammed Mukhashef, Writing by Hadel Al Sayegh, Editing by William Maclean)
