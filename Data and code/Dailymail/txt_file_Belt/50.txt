Pakistan PM travels to China to attend Belt and Road Forum
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/ap/article-4498428/Pakistan-PM-travels-China-attend-Belt-Road-Forum.html
 05:57 BST, 12 May 2017 
 ISLAMABAD (AP) - A spokesman says Pakistani Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif is traveling to Beijing to attend a two-day international gathering promoting China's plan for a vast trade network. Sharif's office says in a statement the premier leaves for China later Friday at the invitation of the President Xi Jinping to attend the event which is part of "Silk Road Economic Belt and the 21st Century Maritime Silk Road" initiative, which was launched by Xi in 2013. The event opens Sunday and will be attended by 27 countries.        FILE - In this Nov. 13, 2016 file photo, Pakistan's Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif, center left, and Army Chief Gen. Raheel Sharif, fourth right, pray near Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Sun Weidong, center after inaugurating a new international trade route during a ceremony at the Gwadar port which links to China's western region, west of Karachi, Pakistan. China's new Silk Road initiative is ramping up as President Donald Trump focuses on domestic issues, downplaying foreign affairs. (AP Photo/Muhammad Yousuf, File)  China is currently working on a new Silk Road of ports, railways and roads in Pakistan to expand trade in a vast arc of countries across Asia, Europe and Africa.
