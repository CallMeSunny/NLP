Two-year-old boy is seriously injured after clambering onto an airport conveyor belt and cutting his arm open

 07:46 BST, 7 June 2018 
A two-year-old boy has been seriously injured after a horror accident at a New Zealand airport.The incident unfolded after the toddler clambered on to a baggage conveyor belt in the departure area of Auckland's international terminal on Thursday afternoon.He was rushed to hospital with a cut to his arm after being provided with first aid at the scene, stuff.co.nz reported.
