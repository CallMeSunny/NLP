Swansea winger Modou Barrow closing in on loan move to Nottingham Forest  
https://www.dailymail.co.uk/sport/football/article-2986916/Swansea-winger-Modou-Barrow-closing-loan-Nottingham-Forest.html
 19:40 BST, 9 March 2015 
Modou Barrow is set to join Nottingham Forest on loan until the end of the season. The 22-year-old is expected to complete the move in the coming days, with Swansea manager Garry Monk keen for the winger to get first-team football. Sportsmail understands an agreement has been reached, but paperwork for the deal has not been completed.
