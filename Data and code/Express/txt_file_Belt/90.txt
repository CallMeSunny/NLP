   The Governor of Mississippi Phil Bryant insisted the US President held a “very strong” and “personal affection” towards Britain.Speaking to Nigel Farage on his LBC show, the politician said he believed the UK was “at the front of the queue” for a trade deal.When asked about the European bloc being made a priority, Mr Bryant said: “I don’t think that’s going to happen.“I don’t want to speak for the President but I can tell you his affection for and support of Great Britain is very strong.
