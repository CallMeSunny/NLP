David Cameron's favourite Buckinghamshire pub bought by Chinese investment firm
https://www.express.co.uk//news/uk/740480/David-Cameron-Chinese-pub-investment-firm-Buckinghamshire
 00:00, Tue, Dec 6, 2016 
  GETTYThe former Prime Minister’s favourite pub has been bought out by a Chinese investment firmThe Plough at Cadsden has been snapped up for a reported £2 million after proving a massive hit with visitors from China.The 16th century country inn near Chequers - the official country retreat used by British prime ministers in Buckinghamshire - was made famous after Mr Cameron dropped in with Chinese President Xi Jinping in October last year.The two leaders each drank a pint of Greene King IPA and tucked into a basket of fish and chips with homemade tartare sauce. Related articles   Wetherspoons boss BLASTS Cameron and Osborne over Brexit   Wetherspoon prints Brexit beer mats that encourage Out vote The establishment became so famous in China it is now to become the model for a chain of British pubs in the People's Republic.Although the pub was not for sale, state-backed enterprise SinoFortone - which has spent billions on British projects and has been linked with a bid for Liverpool football club - reportedly contacted the pub's agents Christie & Co after the state visit.Christie & Co director Neil Morgan said: "The Chinese market has huge potential and many opportunities, so it is one that we will see more activity in over the coming months and years.  GETTYMr Cameron dropped in with Chinese President Xi Jinping in October last year The Chinese market has huge potential and many opportunitiesNeil Morgan"We are seeing more and more Chinese investors in particular coming to us showing an interest both in British hospitality and leisure opportunities, and also in taking classic British themes to China."Dr Peter Zhang, managing director of SinoFortone Investment, said the English pub concept was growing "very fast" in China.He added: "We see bigger opportunities, namely countries lying between China and Europe in where we believe we could also export the UK brand internationally."  GETTYThe Plough at Cadsden has been snapped up for a reported £2 millionThe Plough, which describes itself as "probably the most famous pub in England" as well as being "the pub of choice of prime ministers for many decades" was once owned by EastEnders actress Barbara Windsor.The pub also hit the headlines in 2012 when Mr Cameron and his wife Samantha drove off after a Sunday drink in the beer garden, accidentally leaving behind their eight-year-old daughter, Nancy.The couple, who had been out with two other families, only discovered the mistake when they got home.
