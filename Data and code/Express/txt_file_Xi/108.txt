Where Christmas was BANNED: China’s Christians suffer brutal crackdown over festivities
https://www.express.co.uk//news/world/900102/Christmas-China-christian-persecution-christianity-Xi-Jinping

  GETTY Christmas was banned in China Security officials sent out words of warnings to pastors and worshippers, warning them Christmas trees, Santa Claus, festive hats and any other items relating to Christmas were forbidden.In Heshan, Guangdong, authorities installed CCTV cameras inside a government-run church in a bid to monitor its Christmas festivities in a crackdown on churches by the communist regime. One church in Guangfu was even forced to relocate after suffering repeated persecution, according to China Aid, a human rights organisation. A former pastor said: “A public security agent called me and asked how we celebrated Christmas and how many people would attend, saying we must report to him.
