     The FTSE is barrelling towards a fresh all-time high this MondayConnor Campbell of SpreadExHe said: “The FTSE is barrelling towards a fresh all-time high this Monday, ignoring both the ‘WannaCry’ ransomware attack of last Friday, and a selection of weak Chinese data that saw industrial production drop from 7.6 per cent to 6.5 per cent month-on-month.”Following the shocking cyber attack on Friday, traders have been buying shares in cybersecurity companies. Security software firm Sophos Group was the biggest riser on the FTSE 250, up more than 7 per cent, or 24.4p, to 365.6p.
