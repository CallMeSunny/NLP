   In an editorial published in Beijing’s Global Times, officials took a sharp swipe at Japan for previously trying to flare up a terrestrial dispute over several islands in the East China Sea.But expressing their hopes for “peace and stability”, a comment in the article said: "As we have indeed seen the Japanese side stirring up troubles on the South China Sea issue, we hope the Japanese side can truly play a responsible role for peace and stability in this region."When the drills were confirmed months ago, China’s foreign ministry made a similar threat, urging the countries to “normal transits” while near the Chinese seas.
