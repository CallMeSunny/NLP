US human rights record attacked by CHINA as Trump puts US on verge of 'COLLAPSE'
https://www.express.co.uk//news/world/977108/Trump-UN-Human-Rights-Council-China-United-States-immigration-children-Mexico
 16:39, Wed, Jun 20, 2018 
   Washington withdrew from the United Nations' human rights group yesterday after branding it "hypocritical and self-serving".Nikki Haley, the American ambassador to the UN, also accused the 47-member body of "unending hostility towards Israel".Taking aim at Donald Trump's decision, China said the US' image as a defender of human rights was "on the verge of collapse".The comments come despite Beijing's own poor human rights record, which has repeatedly been criticised by international observers. China has ramped up tensions with Donald Trump (Image: GETTY )The controversial policy seen around 2,000 children divided from their families in six weeks on the southern US border.Mr Trump has faced mounting pressure from both Republicans and Democrats to end his "zero-tolerance" approach to illegal border crossings.Online videos, which have sparked fury worldwide, showed immigrant children being held in concrete-floored cages at detention centres.Human rights have long been a source of tension between China and the US, the world's two largest economies.
