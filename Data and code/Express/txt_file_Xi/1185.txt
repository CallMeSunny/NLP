Armed Greek anarchists call for 'attacks and clashes' during Obama's final overseas tour
https://www.express.co.uk//news/world/731618/Obama-Greek-anarchists-attacks-clashes-final-tour
 00:00, Sat, Nov 12, 2016 
  GettyAnarchists are calling for violence during Mr Obama's Greek tourThe Conspiracy Cells of Fire group is urging anarchists to "spoil the party and sabotage" his visit to Athens on November 15 and 16.The nihilist group has organised a string of bomb attacks on judges, police and other authority figures.Many have received minor injures due to the attacks, but no fatalities. GettyProtesters in Berlin have been hitting out at TrumpAs well as Greece, Mr Obama will visit Germany and Peru on his final major foreign trip as president.The White House said the stop in Greece is Mr Obama's final state visit before leaving office.He will be taken on a tour of the Parthenon, give a speech about globalisation and meet with Prime Minister Alexis Tsipras.   In Germany he will have dinner with Chancellor Angela Merkel and also meet with a group of European leaders, including British Prime Minister Theresa May and French President Francois Hollande.He will attend an Asia economic summit in Peru, and while in Lima will also meet with Chinese President Xi Jinping and Australian Prime Minster Malcolm Turnbull.  GettyDonald Trump and Barack Obama had awkward, but better than to be expected, talks earlier this week  GettyEuropeans have been protesting against Trump's victoryThe trip was meant to be a farewell tour, as well as a chance to bolster support for Mr Obama's agreements on Iran's nuclear programme, trade and climate change.But deputy national security adviser Ben Rhodes says Mr Obama expects Donald Trump to dominate conversation and to face questions about how Mr Trump's election affects those deals.In the case of trade, White House officials acknowledged on Friday it was now up to the president elect and Republican congressional leaders to determine the future of the Trans-Pacific Partnership (TPP).
