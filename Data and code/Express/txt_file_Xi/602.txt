North Korean defector urges world leaders to prepare for ‘DESPERATE’ Kim Jong-un to attack
https://www.express.co.uk//news/world/789402/North-Korea-defector-Thae-Yong-ho-Kim-Jong-un-nuclear-Donald-Trump
 00:00, Sat, Apr 8, 2017 
   Thae Yong-ho, who served as North Korea’s deputy ambassador to the UK before defecting to South Korea last year, alarmingly predicted that the despotic leader would use nuclear weapons against Britain and the United States – unless they “eliminate him first”.The defector also urged world leaders to prepare to deal with a “desperate” Kim, hellbent on maintaining his tyrannical reign through the development of nuclear weapons and intercontinental ballistic missiles (ICBM). GETTYThe claims come amid rising tensions between North Korea and the US  GETTYThe President pledged to bolster defences against North Korea in a meeting with China's presidentThae also argued that the only “real solution to the North Korean nuclear issue is to eliminate Kim Jong-un from the post”.The stark warning comes after US President Donald Trump yesterday pledged to bolster defences against Pyongyang after meeting Xi Jinping, the president of China, for urgent talks in Florida.The US took part in a massive joint naval exercise with Japan and South Korea this week aimed at reducing the threat from North Korean submarines.But the North Korean foreign ministry reacted furiously to the exercise, branded it “reckless” and said it was driving the Korean peninsula to the “brink of war”.Kim Hyong-jun, North Korea’s ambassador to Moscow, warned Trump that his country was ready to respond if challenged by the training drills.He said: "Our army has already said that if there will be even the smallest provocation from the United States during exercises, we are ready to deliver the most ruthless blow.
