NEW BREXIT ERA: Train arrives in UK DIRECTLY from China reopening Silk Road trade route
https://www.express.co.uk//news/uk/755810/Brexit-Article-50-European-Union-Silk-Road-China-London-trade-deal-business
 13:21, Wed, Jan 18, 2017 
   The first direct freight train linking China and the United Kingdom arrived today, just more than 24 hours after Theresa May told the nation to prepare for life outside the EU's single market.The 7,400-mile journey could prove to be a fast and effective way of encouraging monumental amounts of trade between Britain and China in the post-Brexit world.It is thought the train route could transport huge amount of stock in a quicker way than ever before and it is a boost for the Government's Brexit team, who are adamant Britain can prosper without Brussels' involvement. Video of man’s travel hack goes VIRAL   GETTY*ITVBritain is already creating new business relationships outside the EUThe train signifies the reintroduction of the ancient Silk Road and journeys take just 16 days to complete – compared to up to six weeks if containers are sent via boat.The landmark train journey also cost significantly less than waterbourne transportation.  EXPRESSThe train stops in Moscow, Barking and MadridFang Xudong, vice general manager of Tianmeng Industrial Investment, which owns the train, said: "The fast train route between Yiwu and London takes 30 days less than maritime transportation, while only costing a fifth of air transportation."The train, hauling 68 containers of household cargo including clothes, bags and suitcases, has travelled through 14 countries on its route to the UK, including Kazakhstan, Russia and Poland.
