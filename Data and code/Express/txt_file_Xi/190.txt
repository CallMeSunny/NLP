   The missile was fired from the Sinpo region at 10.40pm GMT (6.10am local time) on the communist nation’s east coast and landed into the sea off the Korean peninsula, South Korean military officials confirmed.Seoul said the launch of the medium-range ballistic missile was designed to mimic an attack on their territory from the hermit nation.South Korea’s national security council has announced they will hold an emergency meeting following the latest act of aggression.
