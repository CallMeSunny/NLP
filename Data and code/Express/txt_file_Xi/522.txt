Fifa set to make £4.6billion thanks to Chinese boost
https://www.express.co.uk//news/world/975063/fifa-to-make-billions-thanks-to-Chinese-boost
 01:50, Sat, Jun 16, 2018 
  Fifa set to make £4.6billion after Chinese cash injection (Image: GETTY)Seven of the 20 companies sponsoring the World Cup finals in Russia are Chinese.Their investment has helped bring in 10 per cent more cash than organiser Fifa expected and £1billion more than the 2014 tournament in Brazil produced.Revenue from television rights sales was two per cent higher than Fifa’s £2.25billion target.Fifa’s sponsorship deals have generated £150million more than the projected £1.1billion thanks to a slew of deals from China. Russia’s psychic cat predicts winner  While people were predicting bankruptcies of Fifa or other very dark visions I think the vision is very bright based on figures coming from different parts of the world including Asia.Gianni InfantinoThe governing body’s financial documents also revealed a 233 per cent rise in royalties from football computer software manufacturer EA Sports.The company paid the governing body £120million last year. China’s growing interest in football comes as western companies begin to shun deals with Fifa in the wake of a corruption investigation by the US Justice Department. Fifa has not signed a new sponsor from a western nation since 2011.
