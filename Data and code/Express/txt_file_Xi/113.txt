  GETTYXi Jinping is arriving in the UK on October 19President Xi Jinping today arrives in Britain with his wife ready to receive non-stop lavish treatment for the duration of their four-day stay, with the hope China will agree to pour billions of pounds of investment into UK projects.The Government hopes to seal a "golden" relationship with China and has organised a packed diary of five-star events for Mr Xi and his wife Peng Liyuan, including a stay with the Queen at Buckingham Palace, a luxury state banquet and dining with the Prime Minister at Chequers.Ahead of the visit, China's ambassador to the UK, Liu Xiamong said: "The UK has stated that it will be the Western country that is most open to China."This is a visionary and strategic choice that fully meets Britain's own long-term interest."
