CHINA in FRESH MOVE to surpass Trump military power with £127billion defence BOOST
https://www.express.co.uk//news/world/927655/china-donald-trump-us-military-spending-xi-jinping
 00:44, Tue, Mar 6, 2018 
  GETTYChina has boosted its 2018 defence spending to £127billion According to a bombshell report issued at the opening of China's annual meeting of Parliament, the country’s 2018 defence budget will be 1.11trillion yuan (£127billion).Military chiefs in China reportedly want to make the country’s armed forces the most powerful in the world and have set a target of 8.1 per cent growth.At present, China is the second biggest military spender behind the US.The news has come while China has been developing a whole next-generation battery of weapons including hypersonic missiles and electromagnetic railguns.
