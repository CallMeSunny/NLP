Duchess of Cambridge to return to workKate, 33, already has a busy schedule planned - including an appearance at the opening ceremony of the Rugby World Cup.The Duchess is said to have enjoyed spending the summer with her new daughter and son, Prince George, but is now looking forward to resuming a limited programme of public engagements. Kate's return will kick off with a visit to the Anna Freud Centre in London, as part of a series of appointments designed to focus on mental health issues in children and young adults. This cause is to become a major focus in the Duchess's future programme, which will include visits around the country to charities working with vulnerable children and families, according to palace sources. Kate will also join her husband Prince William, and brother-in-law, Prince Harry - both passionate rugby fans - at the opening ceremony of the Rugby World Cup which starts at Twickenham on September 18. She is also said to be attending several other unconfirmed events for the tournament.  Kate Middleton Duchess of Cambridge pictures Mon, July 16, 2018Kate Middleton, the Duchess of Cambridge in pictures.
