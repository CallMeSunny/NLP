Donald Trump Asia tour MAP: Where is Donald Trump going in Asia?

 12:20, Sat, Nov 4, 2017 
  ExpressThis Donald Trump Asia tour map shows where is Donald Trump going in AsiaDonald Trump is not going to visit the heavily fortified border between North and South Korea during his visit to Asia. But he will discuss the North Korea threat with China as well as key US allies Japan and South Korea next week. Before embarking on the tour of Asia, Mr Trump is visiting Hawaii where he has laid a wreath at the war memorial in Pearl Harbour. The map above shows the dates that Mr Trump will travel to Japan, South Korea, China, Vietnam and the Philippines. The tour begins in JapanMr Trump will meet Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe and the families of Japanese citizens abducted by the North Korean regime.The US President will also meet American and Japanese servicemen based in Japan. Tuesday November 7: Next stop South Korea Mr Trump will meet the new South Korean President Moon Jae-in as well as American and South Korean servicemen.The White House said: “The President will speak at the National Assembly, where he will celebrate the enduring alliance and friendship between the United States and the Republic of Korea, and call on the international community to join together in maximizing pressure on North Korea.”    Wednesday November 8: Beijing, China visit Mr Trump will meet Chinese President Xi Jinping as well as taking part in a series of bilateral, commercial, and cultural events in China. The US President tweeted that he and Melania Trump and are looking forward to being with President Xi and Madame Peng Liyuan for “what will hopefully be a historic trip!”Thursday November 10: On to Danang Vietnam In Danang, Vietnam, Mr Trump will participate in the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) Economic Leaders’ Meeting. The White House said the President will present the American vision of a “free and open Indo-Pacific region” at the APEC CEO summit.   Twitter Donald Trump Asia tour map: The US President tweets about the visit to ChinaFriday November 11: Up to Hanoi, VietnamMr Trump will meet President Tran Dai Quang and other senior Vietnamese leaders in Hanoi. Saturday November 12: Across to Manila in the Philippines President Trump will take part in the Special Gala Celebration Dinner for the 50th Anniversary of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN). Sunday November 13: ASEAN Summit in the Philippines Mr Trump will meet with President Rodrigo Duterte of the Philippines and other leaders at the ASEAN Summit.  Donald Trump's Asia tour in pictures Mon, November 13, 2017Donald
