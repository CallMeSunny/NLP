      Getty ImagesBrigitte Macron news: Brigitte chose red, the Chinese colour of good luck, for her visitThe Macron’s are enjoying a three day tour of China in a trip designed to boost Chinese and French relations.Macron hopes to use this week's visit to China to boost Beijing's ties with Paris, and take over from the UK as China's closest ally in Europe.Mr Macron's first official visit to China is aimed at boosting the global influence of both countries - as France looks to move out of Germany's shadow, and China from the US.The UK had long been seen as China's closest partner in Europe - but since Brexit and the election of Mr Macron, China has increasingly looked to France.     Getty ImagesBrigitte Macron news: Brigitte slipped in to a black coat as she walked around the galleryEmmanuel is the first by the leader of a European country since Xi Jinping secured his grip on power at the communist party congress in October.So far, the couple have seen the famous terracotta warriors, the Big Wild Goose Pagoda, and the Great Mosque of Xian.Brigitte has chosen the colour red - a symbol of good luck in China - on a couple of occasions while in the country, wearing a red coat to visit Xian on her first day.She slipped into a stylish white coat and matching jumper for day two.
