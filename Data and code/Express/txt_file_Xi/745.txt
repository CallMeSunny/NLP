  Getty/ Airbus Defense & Space, 38 NorthNorth Korea could be days away from missile launchThe country has claimed it is launching a rocket with what it called 'an earth observation satellite' in February, but the move is causing worldwide suspicion as many nations believe it is a long range missile test.The country said it will do the launch between Monday and February 25 and satellite images have shown the fuelling process has begun.US intelligence agencies believed North Korea could be ready by the US Super Bowl kickoff on Sunday, which will be Monday in Korea. Activity at the site was consistent with a launch in the time frame given by Pyongyang, the country's capital.US Pacific Command said it was closely monitoring the situation and had many missile defence assets in the region that would provide "a robust defence".
