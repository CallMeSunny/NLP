Kim Jong-un makes rare appearance with reclusive wife Ri Sol-ju in China
https://www.express.co.uk//life-style/life/938196/kim-jong-un-wife-ri-sol-ju-beijing
 10:59, Wed, Mar 28, 2018 
   Kim Jong-un, thought to be 33, has made a visit to China, holding talks with President Xi Jinping, 64.The trip is highly unusual, with Jong-un rarely leaving North Korea. But what is even more surprising is the appearance of Kim’s wife, Ri Sol-ju, who is not often seen in public.Very little is known about the mysterious woman - even her age cannot be confirmed, although she is thought to be between 27 and 32 years old.Ri was seen alongside leader Kim Jong-un regularly in 2012, prompting speculation about whether their pair were in a relationship.Shortly after the speculation started, North Korean state media stated she was the leader’s wife.
