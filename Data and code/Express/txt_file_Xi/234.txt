China conducts LIVE FIRE military drills near Taiwan as World War 3 tensions escalate

  GETTYWorld War 3: Chinese state television showed footage of helicopters firing missilesChinese state television showed footage of helicopters firing missiles during an exercise it said was happening on China's southeast coast.The drills took place off the coast of the city of Quanzhou, in between two groups of islands close to China's coast that Taiwan has controlled since 1949 when defeated Nationalist forces fled to the island at the end of the Chinese civil war.Taiwan on Wednesday denounced the exercises, saying Beijing was using "cheap verbal intimidation and sabre rattling" to threaten the island.It is unclear if these were the same exercises that had been flagged as taking place in the sensitive Taiwan Strait.Chinese state media said the drills were a direct response to "provocations" by Taiwan leaders in what China fears are moves to push for the self-ruled island's formal independence.
