Queen Letizia shows her thrifty side as she recycles entire outfit for exhibition visit
https://www.express.co.uk//life-style/style/1052464/queen-letizia-spain-news-pictures-outfit-madrid-royal-palace-exhibition
 10:04, Fri, Nov 30, 2018 
   Queen Letizia, 46, looked chic in the beige and cream attire, which was made up entirely of items of clothing she has worn to public events before. Mum-of-two Letizia donned a Carolina Herrera stone doublebreasted wool coat which she first wore in December 2016 at 40th anniversary of Zeta Group. She added a white Hugo Boss Banika blouse, a staple which she first stepped out in for the National Innovation and Design Awards in 2015. A pair of Hugo Boss camel pumps that she first wore in November 2016 during a visit to Portugal added a pop of colour.
