‘Fighter-style drama’ Donald Trump ATTACKED in Chinese newspaper as trade tensions RISE

 04:24, Mon, Aug 6, 2018 
  Trump imposed 25 percent tariffs on $34billion of Chinese goods last month (Image: GETTY)The Communist Party’s People’s Daily newspaper criticised the US leader for “starring in his own carefully orchestrated street fighter-style deceitful drama”.The editorial also accused the US of escalating trade tensions with China and turning international trade into a “zero-sum game”.Trump imposed 25 percent tariffs on $34billion (£26billion) of Chinese goods last month, prompting Beijing to respond with equal measures.The US leader has since threatened to impose further 25 percent tariffs on another $200billion (£154billion) worth of Chinese products. Every country on earth wants to take wealth out of the US, always to our detriment.“I say, as they come, tax them. If they don’t want to be taxed, let them make or build the product in the US.“In either event, it means jobs and great wealth.
