    Her vocal talents landed her a place at the Shandong University of Arts aged 14, where she specialised in Chinese folk songs.At 18 years old Peng joined the People’s Liberation Army and was appointed a warrior of arts and culture. She now holds the rank Major General.She later attended the Conservatory of Music in Beijing, during which time she reportedly sent money home to her family and helped poorer students with food stamps.Aged 21, Peng performed on a New Years Eve television special watched by hundreds of millions of viewers. The appearance made her a household name, and she went on to appear on the show regularly.     GETTYPeng is an accomplished singer in her native China  GETTYShe has regularly appeared on state TV for decadesPeng met Xi in 1986 and the couple married in secret just a few months later in 1987. Only top party officials were made aware of the union for the first few years of their marriage.The couple welcomed a daughter, Xi Mingze, in 1992 who went on to study at Harvard University in the US.Since her husband became President in 2013, Peng has scaled back her singing career and has focused on philanthropic work. She is an ambassador for the World Health Organisation and spreads awareness of tuberculosis and HIV/AIDS.
