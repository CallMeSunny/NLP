   The House of Representatives passed the Otto Warmbier North Korea Nuclear Sanctions Act to curb North Korea's access to financial markets.The bill was named after American student Otto Warmbier, who died in June after his release from custody in Pyongyang.It was supported by 415 members of the House, with just two votes against.
